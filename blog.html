Day 3

As part of my German learning, I sent Valentines

Babbel allows you to send anonymous (or not) cards to people on the (two) days leading up to Valentines Day.

Do I participate? Yes I do, I love this holiday, pipe down V-day haters

WRITE ALL THE CARDS FOR EVERYONE

But really, that was part of our assignment - send someone else in the group a Valentine “auf Deutsch”

I perused our chatroom for members that seemed to have just a nugget of enthusiasm about this thing and selected three people to send NOT JUST A V CARD, nay, but a POEM, because unfortunately this is where all my Valentine attention is focused this year, folks. I have nowhere to put all this natural affinity for causeless but true affection for celebrating affection itself. In other words, I like this kitschy, stupid holiday if only because it’s an excuse to tell someone else something nice. If it’s a lie well that’s on you bud.
